Cookie Policy

Mykonos Island Builder — Effective: May 2026

Quick summary: We use minimal cookies. The game itself uses zero cookies. Cookies only come from Google AdSense (ads) and optional analytics. You can accept or reject non-essential cookies below.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function, remember preferences, and serve relevant ads.

2. Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Duration Can Reject?
Essential None required — game runs without cookies N/A N/A
Google AdSense Show relevant ads, prevent ad fraud Up to 13 months Yes (reject in banner)
Google Analytics Understand visitor behavior (optional) Up to 24 months Yes (reject in banner)
LocalStorage Save your island designs (not a cookie) Permanent until cleared Yes (clear browser data)

3. Google AdSense Cookies

Google AdSense may set the following cookies:

  • _gads, _gac — Ad personalization and conversion tracking
  • IDE, test_cookie — Ad delivery optimization
  • ar_debug — Debugging (only when ad debugging is enabled)

For more details: How Google uses advertising cookies.

4. Managing Cookies

You have the right to accept or reject cookies (except essential ones). You can:

  • Use the cookie banner when you first visit (coming soon)
  • Set your browser to block all or certain cookies
  • Delete existing cookies in your browser settings
  • Opt out of Google ads at Google Ads Settings

5. Do Not Track (DNT)

We honor DNT signals. If your browser sends a DNT header, we will not load non-essential analytics or ad personalization.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with a new effective date.

For questions about cookies, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
