What Is This?

Build Your Island

Place houses, churches, windmills, trees, flowers, and more on a 14×14 grid. Create cozy villages, seaside towns, or sprawling estates — it's your island.

🎨

75+ Hand-Drawn Assets

Every building, tree, and decoration is a hand-drawn voxel in classic Mediterranean blue-and-white. From bougainvillea vines to stone lanterns.

📱

Works Everywhere

Desktop, tablet, phone — drag and drop buildings with mouse or touch. Designed for one-handed play on your commute.

💾

Save & Share

Click the 💾 Save button or press S to save your island. Use 📷 Export to download a screenshot and share it with friends!

How to Build

1

Pick a Category

Choose from Terrain, Nature, Props, Water, or Buildings in the toolbar

2

Select an Asset

Click any of the 75+ hand-drawn items — from cypress trees to whitewashed chapels

3

Click to Place

Click anywhere on the island grid. Drag to place multiple. Right-click to erase.

4

Share & Save

Your island is saved automatically. Screenshot and share your creation!
