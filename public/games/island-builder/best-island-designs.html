10 Best Island Design Ideas for 2026

Looking for inspiration for your next island? Here are 10 of the most popular and visually striking island design themes created by the Mykonos Island Builder community. Each comes with tips on how to recreate the look.

1. ??? Classic Greek Village

Beginner Friendly Iconic

The timeless look of whitewashed houses with blue domes, narrow winding paths, and a central church. Cluster buildings tightly for an authentic village feel. Add a windmill on the highest point.

2. ?? Sunset Harbor

Waterfront Romantic

A harbor-focused design with docks, fishing boats, and tavernas lining the water. Use warm-colored roofs and place benches facing west for the "sunset view." Lanterns and torches add evening charm.

3. ?? Organic Farm Island

Nature Peaceful

Dedicate most of your island to agriculture: crop patches, vineyards, olive groves, and garden beds. Scatter farmhouses and barns among the fields. Use fences to divide different crop areas.

4. ? Monastery Retreat

Minimalist Serene

A single large monastery or church complex surrounded by gardens and cypress trees. Keep buildings sparse and let nature dominate. Add a stone path leading up to the main entrance for drama.

5. ?? Fortress Island

Dramatic Historical

Build a walled fortress with towers, gates, and a central keep. Use stone textures and place cannons or defensive structures. The contrast between military architecture and Mediterranean surroundings is striking.

3. ?? Fishing Village

Coastal Cozy

Small colorful houses crowded around a protected bay. Add nets, barrels, boats, and drying racks. The key is clutter ˇŞ make it feel lived-in and working. Use lots of wood textures.

7. ?? Botanical Paradise

Colorful Lush

Maximize flowers, trees, and garden beds. Create themed garden sections: rose garden, herb garden, tropical corner. Place glass greenhouses and gazebos as focal points. Pathways should meander organically.

8. ?? Winery Estate

Elegant Adult

A large villa surrounded by orderly rows of vineyards. Add a tasting room, cellars, and a terrace with views. Use terracotta roofs and stone walls. Cypress trees line the driveway.

9. ??? Beach Resort

Modern Luxury

Beachfront hotels, pools, umbrellas, and loungers. Use bright colors and open spaces. Add a boardwalk, beach bars, and water sports equipment. Palm trees are essential.

10. ?? Night Market Island

Creative Unique

An evening-themed design with stalls, lanterns, string lights, and a performance stage. Use dark ground tiles and lots of light sources. Add seating areas and food stands for atmosphere.
