# Frogger

**Category:** Arcade  
**Difficulty:** Medium

---

## Description

Frogger is a classic 1981 arcade game where you guide a frog across a busy highway and a hazardous river to reach home safely. Dodge cars, trucks, and buses on the road, then hop on logs and turtles to cross the river. One wrong move and you're squashed – it's simple, addictive, and timeless!

---

## How to Play Frogger

1. **Understand the Goal** – Get the frog from the bottom of the screen to one of the five homes at the top.

2. **Cross the Highway** – The bottom half of the screen is a road with moving traffic:
   - **Cars and Trucks** – Move in one direction. Dodge them!
   - **Buses** – Longer vehicles that are harder to slip between
   - **Don't get crushed** – Touching any vehicle costs a life

3. **Cross the River** – The top half is a river with floating objects:
   - **Logs** – Hop on them to float across
   - **Turtles** – They occasionally dive underwater. Time your hops!
   - **Alligators/Crocodiles** – Some have open mouths – avoid those!
   - **If you fall in the water** – You drown (lose a life)

4. **Reach a Home** – Each home slot can only be used once per round. Get all 5 frogs home to complete the level.

5. **Avoid Extra Hazards**:
   - **Snakes** – In some versions, snakes appear in home slots
   - **Time Limit** – You have limited time to reach home
   - **Lily Pads** – In some levels, you must hop across lily pads

## Tips & Strategies

- **Wait for Gaps** – Don't rush into traffic. Wait for a clear gap before crossing.
- **Use the Median** – The narrow strip between the highway and river is a safe resting spot. Use it!
- **Watch the Turtles** – Turtles dive on a timer. If you see them start to dip, get off quickly.
- **Plan Your Route** – Look at the entire river section before leaving the median. Plan which logs to use.
- **Don't Stop on the Road** – Keep moving. Stopping in a lane is asking to get hit.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many levels are there?**
A: The game has multiple rounds with increasing difficulty (faster traffic, more hazards).

**Q: What are the flies for?**
A: Eating flies (in some versions) gives bonus points.

**Q: Why is this game so hard?**
A: Frogger requires precise timing and patience. It's challenging but fair – every death teaches you something.
