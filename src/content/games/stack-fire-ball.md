# Stack Fire Ball

**Category:** Arcade  
**Difficulty:** Medium

---

## Description

Stack Fire Ball is an addictive physics-based stacking game. Drop bouncing fire balls from the top of the screen and stack them as high as possible. But be careful – if the balls fall off the platform, you lose them! Precision and timing are everything in this fiery challenge.

---

## How to Play Stack Fire Ball

1. **Tap to Drop** – Tap or click to release fire balls from the dispenser at the top.

2. **Aim for the Platform** – The balls bounce and roll. Try to land them on the rotating platform below.

3. **Stack Higher** – Successfully landed balls stack up. The higher you stack, the more points you score.

4. **Watch the Platform Size** – The platform shrinks after each successful stack, making it harder to land balls.

5. **Avoid Falling Balls** – Balls that fall off the platform are lost. Lose too many and the game ends.

6. **Collect Power-ups** – Occasionally, power-ups appear that give you extra balls or slow down the platform rotation.

## Tips & Strategies

- **Tap in Bursts** – Don't hold the tap; release balls in controlled bursts for better accuracy.
- **Aim for the Center** – Dropping balls near the center reduces the chance of them rolling off.
- **Time the Rotation** – Watch the platform rotation pattern. Drop balls when the wide side faces up.
- **Use the Walls** – Bouncing balls off side walls can help guide them onto the platform.
- **Save Power-ups** – Don't use multi-ball power-ups early. Save them for when the platform is small and tricky.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How high can I stack?**
A: There's no theoretical limit, but the shrinking platform makes extremely high stacks very difficult.

**Q: Why did my ball bounce off?**
A: The physics simulate real bouncing. Try angling your drops or dropping from a slightly different position.

**Q: Can I change the ball type?**
A: Some versions unlock different ball skins as you progress.
