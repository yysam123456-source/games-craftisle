# Typing Speed Test

**Category:** Puzzle  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

How fast can you type? The Typing Speed Test measures your Words Per Minute (WPM) and accuracy. Type the displayed text as quickly and accurately as possible. Great for improving your typing skills and tracking your progress over time.

---

## How to Play Typing Speed Test

1. **Choose a Test Mode** – Select duration (30s, 60s, 120s) or word count mode.

2. **Start Typing** – Click the input box and start typing the displayed text exactly as shown.

3. **Match the Text** – Type each character correctly. The test highlights:
   - **Green** – Correct characters
   - **Red** – Mistakes (you can usually backspace to fix them)

4. **Finish the Test** – Complete the text (or time runs out) to see your results.

5. **View Your Stats** – Results show:
   - **WPM (Words Per Minute)** – Your typing speed
   - **Accuracy %** – Percentage of correct keystrokes
   - **Errors** – Total mistakes made

6. **Try Again** – Practice makes perfect! Retake the test to improve.

## Tips & Strategies

- **Focus on Accuracy First** – Speed comes naturally. It's better to type slowly and correctly than fast with many errors.
- **Use All Fingers** – Learn proper touch-typing (home row position). It's faster long-term.
- **Don't Look at the Keyboard** – Train yourself to type by feel. Looking slows you down.
- **Practice Common Words** – English has high-frequency words (the, and, ing). Master these first.
- **Relax Your Hands** – Tension slows you down. Keep your hands relaxed and maintain a steady rhythm.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What's a good WPM score?**
A: 40 WPM is average; 60+ WPM is fast; 80+ WPM is very fast. Professional typists reach 100+ WPM.

**Q: Does punctuation count?**
A: Yes – commas, periods, and capitalization all count toward accuracy.

**Q: Can I practice specific keys?**
A: Some versions offer lessons focusing on specific fingers or key groups.
