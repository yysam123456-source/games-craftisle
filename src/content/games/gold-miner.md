# Gold Miner

**Category:** Arcade  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

Gold Miner is a classic arcade-style game where you control a claw machine to mine gold and treasures from underground. Time your claw drops carefully, haul up valuable items, and reach the target amount before time runs out. It's all about timing, strategy, and a little bit of luck!

---

## How to Play Gold Miner

1. **Drop the Claw** – Press Space or click/tap to lower the claw.

2. **Grab Items** – The claw automatically grabs the first item it touches. It then retracts, pulling the item up.

3. **Different Items Have Different Values**:
   - **Gold Nuggets** – High value, moderate weight
   - **Diamonds** – Very high value, very light (fast to pull up)
   - **Rocks** – Low value, very heavy (slow to pull up)
   - **TNT Barrels** – Negative value! Avoid them or they'll cost you money
   - **Pouches/Piggy Banks** – Medium value, medium weight
   - **Animals (mice, etc.)** – Wiggle and shake, making them harder to pull up

4. **Reach the Target** – Each level has a money target. Reach it before time runs out to advance.

5. **Buy Upgrades** – Between levels, spend your earned money on:
   - **Stronger Rope** – Pulls items up faster
   - **Longer Claw** – Reaches farther
   - **TNT** – Explodes rocks to reveal better items
   - **Lucky Clover** – Increases luck

## Tips & Strategies

- **Prioritize Diamonds** – They're worth the most and light to pull. Go for them whenever possible.
- **Avoid Rocks When Time Is Tight** – Rocks take forever to pull up. Skip them if you're racing the clock.
- **Use TNT on Rocks** – If you must grab a rock, use TNT to destroy it and reveal what's underneath.
- **Watch the Animal Items** – They wiggle, which slows down the claw. Be patient.
- **Upgrade Strategically** – Strength upgrades are usually the best first investment.

A: The game features many levels with increasing targets and new items.

**Q: Can I replay a level?**
A: Yes – if you fail a level, you can retry it.
