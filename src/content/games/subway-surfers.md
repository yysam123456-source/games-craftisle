# Subway Surfers

**Category:** Arcade  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

The world's most downloaded mobile game comes to your browser! Subway Surfers is an endless runner where you dash through subway tracks, dodge trains, collect coins, and outrun the grumpy Inspector and his dog. Colorful graphics, smooth controls, and addictive gameplay make it an instant classic.

---

## How to Play Subway Surfers

1. **Start Running** – Swipe up or press Space to start running.

2. **Dodge Obstacles**:
   - **Swipe Up / Space** – Jump over low barriers and trains
   - **Swipe Down / Down Arrow** – Roll under overhead obstacles
   - **Swipe Left/Right / ← →** – Switch between the three lanes

3. **Collect Coins** – Gather gold coins along the track. They're used to buy upgrades and characters.

4. **Grab Power-ups** – Special items appear on the track:
   - **Jetpack** – Fly above the track, collecting coins from the air
   - **Super Sneakers** – Jump much higher than normal
   - **Magnet** – Attracts all nearby coins
   - **Multiplier** – Increases your score multiplier
   - **Hoverboard** – Protects you from one crash (automatically activates upon hitting an obstacle)

5. **Complete Missions** – Each run has 3 missions (e.g., "Collect 500 coins", "Roll 20 times"). Completing them levels up your multiplier.

6. **Use Keys** – If you crash, you can spend a key to continue from where you left off.

## Tips & Strategies

- **Focus on the Near Future** – Look 2-3 seconds ahead rather than right in front of you.
- **Don't Always Switch Lanes** – Staying in one lane is sometimes safer than weaving.
- **Save the Hoverboard** – Don't use it immediately. Save it for when things get chaotic.
- **Complete Daily Challenges** – They give extra rewards and help you progress faster.
- **Upgrade Power-ups** – Spend coins to make power-ups last longer or appear more frequently.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many characters are there?**
A: The game features many unlockable characters, each with a unique look.

**Q: What are the paint cans for?**
A: Painting trains gives you bonus coins and helps with missions.

**Q: Can I play without an internet connection?**
