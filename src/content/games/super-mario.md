# Super Mario

**Category:** Arcade  
**Difficulty:** Medium

---

## Description

The most famous plumber in gaming history returns! Play a Super Mario-inspired platformer in your browser. Jump on Goombas, collect coins, power up with mushrooms and fire flowers, and rescue the princess from Bowser. Classic platforming action that never gets old.

---

## How to Play Super Mario

1. **Move Right** – Mario automatically moves forward in some versions, or use arrow keys to move left/right.

2. **Jump on Enemies** – Press the jump button (Space, Z, or tap) to jump. Land on top of enemies to defeat them.

3. **Collect Coins** – Gather coins scattered throughout the level. 100 coins = 1 extra life.

4. **Get Power-ups**:
   - **Super Mushroom** – Makes Mario grow bigger. Gives an extra hit point.
   - **Fire Flower** – Lets Mario throw fireballs at enemies.
   - **Star** – Temporary invincibility. Mario can destroy anything while under the star's effect.
   - **1-Up Mushroom** – Grants an extra life (green mushroom).

5. **Reach the Flagpole** – At the end of each level, touch the flagpole. The higher you grab, the more points you score.

6. **Enter Warp Pipes** – Some pipes lead to bonus areas or secret exits.

7. **Defeat Bowser** – In final levels, jump over or on Bowser to defeat him and rescue the princess!

## Tips & Strategies

- **Don't Rush** – Take your time to spot hidden blocks and coins.
- **Hit Question Mark Blocks** – Jump up and hit blocks with ? on them. They contain coins and power-ups.
- **Remember: Invisible Blocks Exist** – Some blocks are invisible until you hit them.
- **Use Fire Flower Wisely** – Fireballs can defeat most enemies, but be careful not to get hit.
- **Look for Secret Exits** – Some levels have hidden paths that unlock new worlds on the map.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many worlds are there?**
A: The game features multiple worlds, each with several levels.

**Q: What happens when I get hit?**
A: If you're Super Mario, you shrink to Small Mario. If you're Small Mario, you die and lose a life.

**Q: Can I play as Luigi?**
A: Some versions offer Luigi as a selectable character with slightly different physics.
