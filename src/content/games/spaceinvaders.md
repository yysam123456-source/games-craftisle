# Space Invaders

The classic 1978 arcade game that started it all! Defend Earth from waves of descending alien invaders.

## How to Play

- **Left/Right Arrow** keys to move your ship
- **Space** to fire lasers
- Destroy all aliens before they reach the bottom
- Each wave gets faster and harder
