# Pong

**Category:** Arcade  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

The grandfather of all video games – Pong! Play the classic 1972 arcade game right in your browser. Control a paddle and bounce the ball past your opponent to score points. Simple, addictive, and historically significant – every gamer should play this at least once.

---

## How to Play Pong

1. **Choose Game Mode** – Select single player (vs AI) or two player (vs friend on same keyboard).

2. **Control Your Paddle** – Use ↑ ↓ arrow keys (Player 1) or W/S keys (Player 2) to move your paddle up and down.

3. **Serve the Ball** – The ball starts in the center. It launches in a random direction after a brief pause.

4. **Bounce the Ball** – Hit the ball with your paddle to send it toward the opponent's side.

5. **Score Points** – When the ball passes your opponent's paddle, you score 1 point.

6. **First to 11 Wins** – The game ends when one player reaches 11 points.

7. **Increasing Speed** – The ball speeds up slightly after each paddle hit, making rallies more intense.

## Tips & Strategies

- **Aim for the Corners** – Hit the ball with the edge of your paddle to send it at an angle the opponent won't expect.
- **Watch the Ball Trajectory** – The ball's angle after bouncing depends on where it hits your paddle.
- **Move Early** – Start moving toward the ball's predicted path before it gets close.
- **Don't Overcommit** – Stay centered so you can react to both high and low shots.
- **In Two-Player Mode** – Use psychology! Vary your timing to throw off your opponent.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I make the AI harder?**
A: The AI difficulty increases as you score more points in single-player mode.

**Q: Can I play against a friend online?**
A: The web version supports local two-player only. Sit together and share the keyboard!

**Q: Why is this game so famous?**
A: Pong (1972) was one of the earliest arcade video games and helped launch the entire gaming industry.
