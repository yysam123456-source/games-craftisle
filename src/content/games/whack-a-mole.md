# Whack-a-Mole

**Category:** Arcade  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

The classic carnival game comes to your browser! Moles pop up randomly from holes – whack them as fast as you can to score points. But watch out for bombs and pretend moles that trick you! Fast reflexes and sharp eyes are the keys to a high score.

---

## How to Play Whack-a-Mole

1. **Start the Game** – Click or tap "Start" to begin. Moles will start appearing from holes.

2. **Whack the Moles** – Click or tap on a mole as soon as it pops up. Each successful whack scores points.

3. **Avoid Bombs** – Some holes spawn bombs instead of moles. Clicking a bomb deducts points!

4. **Watch for Special Moles** – Golden moles give bonus points. Speedy moles disappear extra fast.

5. **Beat the Clock** – You have limited time (usually 30-60 seconds) to score as many points as possible.

6. **Advance Rounds** – Later rounds have faster moles and more tricky patterns.

## Tips & Strategies

- **Keep the Cursor Centered** – Position your mouse/cursor in the middle of the grid for the fastest reaction.
- **Don't Chase Every Mole** – If two moles appear far apart, pick one and go for it. Don't waste time moving between them.
- **Learn the Patterns** – Moles often appear in semi-predictable sequences. Watch for patterns.
- **Ignore Bombs Completely** – Focus only on moles. Trying to avoid bombs can slow you down.
- **Play in Fullscreen** – More screen space means less mouse movement between holes.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long is each round?**
A: Typically 30-60 seconds per round, depending on the version.

**Q: Can I play on mobile?**
A: Yes – touch controls work great. Tap moles as they appear.

**Q: Is there a two-player mode?**
A: Some versions offer competitive two-player on the same device.
