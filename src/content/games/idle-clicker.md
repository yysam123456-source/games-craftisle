# Idle Clicker

**Category:** Arcade  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

The ultimate idle/clicker game! Click to earn coins, then use those coins to buy upgrades that generate coins automatically. Watch your wealth grow even when you're not actively playing. With dozens of upgrades, achievements, and prestige systems, there's always a new goal to chase.

---

## How to Play Idle Clicker

1. **Click to Earn** – Click or tap the main button to earn coins manually.

2. **Buy Upgrades** – Spend coins on upgrades that increase your coins per click (CPC) or coins per second (CPS).

3. **Automate Income** – Purchase auto-generators (minions, buildings, factories) that earn coins even when you're not clicking.

4. **Unlock New Tiers** – As you accumulate wealth, new upgrade tiers become available with exponentially higher rewards.

5. **Prestige/Restart** – Once you've made significant progress, use the Prestige function to reset your progress in exchange for permanent multipliers.

6. **Chase Achievements** – Complete specific goals (click X times, earn Y coins, own Z upgrades) to unlock achievement bonuses.

## Tips & Strategies

- **Balance Clicking and Automation** – Early game: click actively. Mid game: invest in automation. Late game: prestige.
- **Don't Prestige Too Early** – Wait until you have a substantial amount saved up for a meaningful multiplier.
- **Check Back Often** – Idle games reward frequent check-ins. Even 30 seconds every hour helps.
- **Go for Achievements** – They provide permanent bonuses that make future runs faster.
- **Use the Offline Earnings** – Most idle games calculate earnings while you're away. Collect them when you return!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to keep the game open?**
A: No – most idle games continue earning while closed (offline earnings). Check back periodically!

**Q: What does 'Prestige' do?**
A: Prestige resets your progress but gives you a permanent multiplier for future runs. It's how you progress long-term.

**Q: Is there an end to the game?**
A: Idle games are theoretically endless – numbers just get bigger and bigger!
