# Zuma

**Category:** Puzzle  
**Difficulty:** Medium

---

## Description

Zuma is an addictive marble-shooting puzzle game. Control a stone frog that shoots colored marbles into a chain of rolling marbles. Match 3+ marbles of the same color to eliminate them before they reach the skull at the end of the path. With its Aztec theme, soothing music, and increasingly challenging levels, Zuma is a true classic.

---

## How to Play Zuma

1. **Aim the Frog** – Move your mouse or use arrow keys to rotate the stone frog.

2. **Shoot Marbles** – Click or press Space to fire the current marble toward the chain.

3. **Match 3+ Marbles** – When you shoot a marble into a chain and create a group of 3+ same-colored marbles, they explode and disappear.

4. **Stop the Chain** – The chain of marbles slowly rolls forward along a fixed path. If any marble reaches the skull at the end, you lose a life.

5. **Create Combos** – Eliminating a section of the chain can cause the two ends to connect, often creating chain reactions.

6. **Use Power Gems**:
   - **Reverse Gem** – Pushes the chain backward
   - **Slow Gem** – Slows the chain's movement
   - **Bomb Gem** – Explodes and clears nearby marbles
   - **Accuracy Gem** – Makes aiming easier

7. **Clear the Board** – Eliminate all marbles to complete the level.

## Tips & Strategies

- **Aim for Gaps** – Shooting into a gap in the chain can separate it, causing a combo.
- **Don't Waste Shots** – Take your time to aim. A poorly placed marble can make things worse.
- **Use Wall Bounces** – The frog's shots can bounce off walls. Use this to reach difficult angles.
- **Prioritize Power Gems** – They appear periodically. Grab them – they can save you from losing.
- **Watch the Chain Speed** – The chain speeds up as it gets shorter. Be prepared for a fast finish!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many levels are there?**
A: The game features many levels across multiple temples, each with unique layouts.

**Q: Can I swap the current marble?**
A: Yes – right-click or press the swap button to change the current marble with the next one.

**Q: What happens if the chain reaches the skull?**
A: You lose a life. Lose all lives and it's game over.
