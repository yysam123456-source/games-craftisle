# Brick Breaker

**Category:** Arcade  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

The classic Brick Breaker game reimagined for the web. Control a paddle at the bottom of the screen and bounce a ball to break all the bricks at the top. Collect power-ups, avoid losing the ball, and clear every level. Simple to learn, hard to master – the ultimate time killer.

---

## How to Play Brick Breaker

1. **Launch the Ball** – Click or tap to release the ball from the paddle at the start.

2. **Move the Paddle** – Use arrow keys (← →) or touch/mouse to move the paddle left and right.

3. **Bounce the Ball** – The ball bounces off the paddle, walls, and bricks. Angle your paddle to control the ball direction.

4. **Break All Bricks** – Each brick takes one or more hits to destroy. Clear all bricks to advance.

5. **Collect Power-ups** – When certain bricks break, power-ups drop. Catch them with your paddle:
   - **Wide Paddle** – Expands your paddle
   - **Multi-Ball** – Adds extra balls
   - **Fire Ball** – Ball passes through bricks
   - **Slow Ball** – Reduces ball speed
   - **Extra Life** – Gains an additional ball

6. **Don't Lose the Ball** – If the ball falls below the paddle, you lose a life. Game over when all lives are gone.

## Tips & Strategies

- **Control the Angle** – Hit the ball with the edge of the paddle to send it bouncing at sharp angles.
- **Prioritize Power-ups** – Always try to catch helpful power-ups, especially Multi-Ball.
- **Aim for Weak Spots** – Target bricks that are hard to reach once the easy ones are gone.
- **Save Lives for Later Levels** – Play conservatively in early levels to stock up on lives.
- **Use Walls** – Bank shots off the side walls to reach high corners.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many levels are there?**
A: The game features multiple levels with increasing brick density and special brick types.

**Q: What do colored bricks mean?**
A: Different colors typically indicate different hit points – darker bricks may need 2-3 hits.

**Q: Can I pause the game?**
A: Yes – press ESC or the pause button to pause at any time.
