# Bubble Shooter

**Category:** Puzzle  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

Bubble Shooter is a colorful and addictive puzzle game. Aim and shoot colored bubbles to match three or more of the same color. Clear all bubbles from the board to advance to the next level. With simple controls and increasingly challenging puzzles, it's easy to learn but hard to put down!

---

## How to Play Bubble Shooter

1. **Aim the Shooter** – Move your mouse or finger to aim the bubble shooter at the bottom.

2. **Shoot Bubbles** – Click or tap to fire the current bubble toward the ceiling.

3. **Match 3+ Bubbles** – When 3 or more bubbles of the same color connect, they pop and disappear.

4. **Plan Your Shots** – Use the walls to bounce bubbles into hard-to-reach places.

5. **Clear the Board** – Eliminate all bubbles to complete the level.

6. **Watch the Line** – A new row of bubbles appears when you run out of shots or after a certain number of turns.

7. **Game Over** – If bubbles reach the bottom line, the game ends.

## Tips & Strategies

- **Aim for Clusters** – Target existing groups of the same color to create chain reactions.
- **Use Wall Bounces** – Bank shots off the side walls to reach tricky spots.
- **Save the Same Color** – If you have two bubble colors queued that match the same cluster, you can clear a large section in two shots.
- **Work from the Edges** – Clearing bubbles from the sides can cause large sections to detach and fall.
- **Don't Waste Shots** – Every missed shot adds to the board. Make each shot count.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many levels are there?**
A: The game features unlimited levels with increasing difficulty.

**Q: Can I change the bubble color before shooting?**
A: Some versions let you swap the current bubble with the next one.

**Q: What happens when the board fills up?**
A: If bubbles reach the bottom, the game ends. Try to keep the board clear!
