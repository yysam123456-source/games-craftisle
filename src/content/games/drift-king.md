# Drift King

**Category:** Racing  
**Difficulty:** Medium

---

## Description

Drift King is an adrenaline-pumping drifting racing game. Slide your car around sharp corners, earn drift points, and become the ultimate drift champion. With realistic physics, multiple tracks, and upgradeable cars, it's the perfect game for racing enthusiasts who love the art of drifting.

---

## How to Play Drift King

1. **Choose Your Car** – Select from a garage of sports cars. Each has different handling, speed, and drift characteristics.

2. **Pick a Track** – Race on various tracks: city streets, mountain passes, race circuits, or drift parks.

3. **Master the Drift** – To initiate a drift:
   - **Handbrake method** – Tap the handbrake (Space) while turning
   - **Power-over method** – Accelerate hard while turning at high speed
   - **Scandi flick** – Flick the steering in the opposite direction before the turn, then turn into the corner

4. **Maintain the Drift** – Counter-steer (steer in the direction of the drift) to control the slide. Modulate throttle to sustain the drift.

5. **Earn Drift Points** – The longer and more angled your drift, the more points you score. Perfect drifts fill your multiplier bar.

6. **Upgrade Your Car** – Use earned credits to upgrade:
   - **Engine** – More power = faster acceleration
   - **Tires** – Better grip for more controllable drifts
   - **Suspension** – Improved handling and weight transfer
   - **Weight** – Lighter cars are more agile

## Tips & Strategies

- **Practice Throttle Control** – Drifting is about balancing gas and steering. Too much gas = spin out; too little = straighten out.
- **Use Manual Transmission** – Shifting manually gives you more control over power delivery.
- **Find the Clipping Point** – Aim to get as close as possible to the apex of each corner.
- **Chain Your Drifts** – Linking multiple corners into one continuous drift maximizes points.
- **Watch Replays** – Study your replay to see where you can improve your line and angle.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What's the best car for beginners?**
A: Start with a balanced car that has moderate power and good traction. Avoid high-horsepower cars until you master basics.

**Q: How do I unlock new tracks?**
A: Complete challenges and reach drift score milestones to unlock new tracks.

**Q: Is there a career mode?**
A: Yes – progress through drift events, boss battles, and time attacks to become the Drift King.
