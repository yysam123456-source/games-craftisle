# Snake - Complete Game Guide

## 🎮 Game Overview

Snake is one of the most recognizable and beloved arcade games of all time. Originally popularized by Nokia cell phones in the late 1990s, the concept dates back to the 1976 arcade game Blockade. The premise is deliciously simple: control a growing snake, eat food to grow longer, but don't crash into walls or your own tail. Easy to learn, impossible to master—Snake has hooked generations of players.

## 📜 History & Cultural Impact

- **1976**: Blockade arcade game establishes the snake genre
- **1997**: Nokia 6110 includes Snake as a pre-installed game
- **2000s**: Snake becomes synonymous with mobile gaming
- **2010s**: Snake reincarnated in countless web and mobile versions
- **Cultural Icon**: The Nokia Snake is considered one of the most important games in mobile history

## 🎯 How to Play

### Basic Rules
1. **Control the Snake**: Use arrow keys or swipe to change direction
2. **Eat Food**: Collect food items to grow longer and score points
3. **Avoid Crashing**: Don't hit walls or your own body
4. **Grow Longer**: Each food item increases your snake's length
5. **Survive**: Last as long as possible without crashing

### Controls
- **Keyboard**: Arrow keys (up, down, left, right)
- **Touch/Mobile**: Swipe in the direction you want to go
- **Gamepad**: D-pad or analog stick

### Scoring
- Each food item: +10 points
- Bonus food (rare): +50 points
- Length bonus: +1 point per segment every 100 points

## 🧠 Strategy Guide

### Beginner Tips
1. **Plan Your Route**: Always look ahead to avoid trapping yourself
2. **Use the Whole Screen**: Don't confine yourself to a small area
3. **Don't Rush**: Slow and steady wins the race
4. **Watch Your Tail**: It follows you—don't get cornered by it

### Advanced Strategies

#### Spiral Technique
- Gradually spiral inward as you grow longer
- Maximizes space usage
- Reduces risk of trapping yourself

#### Wall Riding
- Stay close to walls to force food into open areas
- Makes it easier to predict safe paths
- Good for intermediate players

#### Speed Control
- Some versions have speed increases as you grow
- Learn to handle faster speeds gradually
- Don't panic when the game speeds up

### Common Mistakes
- Moving too quickly without planning
- Trapping yourself in corners
- Not paying attention to tail position
- Getting greedy for food in dangerous positions

## 🎲 Game Variations

- **Classic Snake**: Original Nokia-style gameplay
- **Speed Snake**: Gradually increasing speed
- **Multiplayer Snake**: Compete against other players
- **3D Snake**: Snake in three dimensions
- **Snake.io**: Modern multiplayer browser version
