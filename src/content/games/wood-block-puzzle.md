# Wood Block Puzzle

**Category:** Puzzle  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

Wood Block Puzzle is a calming yet challenging tetris-style puzzle game. Drag wooden blocks onto a 10×10 grid, filling rows and columns to clear them. Unlike Tetris, blocks don't fall – you place them strategically. With no time limit and a warm wood aesthetic, it's perfect for relaxing while keeping your brain engaged.

---

## How to Play Wood Block Puzzle

1. **See Your Blocks** – Three wooden block pieces appear at the bottom of the screen.

2. **Drag a Block onto the Grid** – Place any of the three pieces onto the 10×10 grid. The piece must fit entirely within empty cells.

3. **Clear Rows and Columns** – When a row or column is completely filled, it disappears and you score points.

4. **Continue Placing** – After placing a piece, new pieces appear at the bottom. Keep placing!

5. **Game Over** – The game ends when none of the three available pieces can fit on the grid.

6. **Aim for Combos** – Clearing multiple rows/columns at once gives bonus points.

## Tips & Strategies

- **Save Space for 1×1 Blocks** – Always leave some small gaps; you'll eventually get single-square pieces.
- **Prioritize Row Completion** – Focus on completing rows (or columns) that are almost full.
- **Think Ahead** – Look at all three available pieces before placing any. Plan your moves.
- **Corner Strategy** – Try to keep corners clear; they're the hardest places to fit blocks later.
- **Don't Rush** – There's no time limit. Take your time to find the optimal placement.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is this the same as Tetris?**
A: Similar concept, but Wood Block Puzzle has no falling pieces and no time pressure. It's more strategic and relaxing.

**Q: Can I rotate blocks?**
A: Most versions allow rotating blocks before placing them.

**Q: What's a good high score?**
A: Scores over 500 are solid; over 1,000 is excellent. The theoretical maximum is much higher!
