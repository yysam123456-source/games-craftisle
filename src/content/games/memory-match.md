# Memory Match

**Category:** Puzzle  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

Memory Match (also known as Concentration) is a classic card-matching game that tests your memory and focus. Flip cards to find matching pairs – but be careful, the cards flip back over if you don't find a match! With cute icons and multiple grid sizes, it's fun for all ages.

---

## How to Play Memory Match

1. **Choose Grid Size** – Select a difficulty: 4×4 (easy), 4×5 (medium), 6×6 (hard), or larger.

2. **Flip a Card** – Click or tap any card to reveal its icon/image.

3. **Flip a Second Card** – Try to find the matching card. If the two cards match, they stay face-up.

4. **If No Match** – Both cards flip back face-down. Try to remember their positions!

5. **Clear the Board** – Find all matching pairs to complete the level.

6. **Beat Your Time** – Try to finish as fast as possible with the fewest moves.

## Tips & Strategies

- **Focus on One Section** – Don't try to memorize the whole board at once. Focus on a small area first.
- **Say It Out Loud** – Verbally noting "apple in top-left, cat in bottom-right" can help memory.
- **Use a Systematic Approach** – Flip cards in a pattern (left-to-right, top-to-bottom) to ensure you've seen everything.
- **Remember Mismatches** – When two cards don't match, make a mental note: "Card A is X, Card B is Y."
- **Practice Makes Perfect** – Your memory will improve with each game. Don't get discouraged!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Are the card positions random?**
A: Yes – every new game shuffles the cards randomly.

**Q: Can I play with a friend?**
A: The classic version is single-player, but you can take turns and compete for the best time.

**Q: Do the images repeat across games?**
A: Most versions use a set of icons but shuffle their positions each game.
