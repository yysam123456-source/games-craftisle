# Merge Tower

**Category:** Strategy  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

Merge Tower is a unique blend of tower defense and merge mechanics. Place units on the battlefield, merge identical units to create stronger ones, and defend your tower from waves of enemies. Simple to learn but with deep strategic possibilities – every merge decision matters!

---

## How to Play Merge Tower

1. **Place Units** – Drag and drop units onto the battlefield grid.

2. **Merge Units** – Drag one unit onto an identical unit of the same level to merge them into a stronger unit. For example, two level-1 archers = one level-2 archer.

3. **Defend the Tower** – Enemies will spawn and march toward your tower. Your units automatically attack enemies within range.

4. **Upgrade Your Units** – Higher-level units have more damage, range, or special abilities.

5. **Use Abilities** – Some units have special abilities (freeze, splash damage, etc.). Learn what each unit type does.

6. **Survive Waves** – Each wave gets harder. Earn gold from defeating enemies to buy more units.

7. **Win the Level** – Defeat all enemy waves to complete the level and unlock the next one.

## Tips & Strategies

- **Don't Merge Too Early** – Sometimes it's better to have multiple low-level units covering different areas than one high-level unit in one spot.
- **Cover All Paths** – Make sure your units cover all enemy approach routes.
- **Save Gold for Key Upgrades** – Don't spend all your gold immediately. Save some for crucial upgrades before boss waves.
- **Experiment with Layouts** – Different unit arrangements work better for different maps.
- **Balance Offense and Defense** – Some units are better at damage; others are better at slowing enemies. Mix both types.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many unit types are there?**
A: The game features multiple unit types: archers, warriors, mages, cannons, and more.

**Q: Can I undo a merge?**
A: No – merging is permanent. Think carefully before merging!

**Q: What's the maximum unit level?**
A: Units can typically be merged up to level 5-6, depending on the version.
