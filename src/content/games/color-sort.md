# Color Sort Puzzle

**Category:** Puzzle  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

Color Sort Puzzle is a deceptively simple brain teaser. You have several tubes filled with colored water, and your goal is to sort all colors so each tube contains only one color. Sounds easy? It gets tricky fast! With hundreds of levels and no time limit, it's the perfect puzzle game to relax and challenge your brain.

---

## How to Play Color Sort Puzzle

1. **Understand the Goal** – Each tube starts with a mix of colors. Your job is to pour water between tubes until each tube contains only one color.

2. **Tap a Tube** – Select the top color of a tube to pick it up.

3. **Pour Into Another Tube** – Tap a different tube to pour the color you're holding. You can only pour if:
   - The target tube is empty, OR
   - The top color of the target tube matches the color you're pouring

4. **Use the Undo Button** – Made a mistake? Tap undo to reverse your last move.

5. **Add Extra Tubes** – Some levels give you empty "helper" tubes. Use them to temporarily store colors.

6. **Complete the Level** – All tubes sorted = level complete. Advance to the next puzzle!

## Tips & Strategies

- **Work from the Bottom** – Focus on getting one complete tube first. It's easier to sort the rest afterward.
- **Use Empty Tubes Wisely** – Don't fill your extra tubes too early; you'll need them for complex moves.
- **Think 3 Moves Ahead** – Plan your sequence before pouring, especially in later levels.
- **Don't Rush** – There's no time limit. Take your time to find the optimal solution.
- **Restart if Stuck** – Some levels have multiple solutions. If you're truly stuck, restart and try a different approach.
