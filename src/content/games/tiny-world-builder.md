# Tiny World Builder

**Category:** Building  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

Tiny World Builder is a charming miniature landscape creation game. Build cute little worlds with tiny houses, trees, roads, and decorations. It's like playing with digital toy blocks – simple, relaxing, and endlessly creative. Perfect for all ages.

---

## How to Play Tiny World Builder

1. **Choose a Base** – Select a terrain type (grass, sand, snow, etc.) as the foundation of your tiny world.

2. **Place Buildings** – Drag and drop houses, shops, castles, and other structures onto the map.

3. **Add Nature** – Place trees, flowers, bushes, and water features to bring your world to life.

4. **Lay Roads** – Connect buildings with roads, paths, and bridges for a realistic town layout.

5. **Decorate** – Add lamps, benches, fences, and seasonal decorations to give your world character.

6. **Populate** – Place tiny people, animals, and vehicles to make the world feel alive.

7. **Save & Share** – Take a screenshot or export your tiny world to show off your creation.

## Tips & Strategies

- **Start Small** – Build a cozy village first before expanding to a full town.
- **Theme Your World** – Pick a theme (medieval, modern, fantasy, winter) and stick to it for a cohesive look.
- **Use Height Variation** – Place buildings on hills or near water for more interesting compositions.
- **Add Seasonal Touches** – Snow on roofs, autumn leaves, or cherry blossoms add charm.
- **Don't Overcrowd** – Leave some open space for paths and gardens.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I undo a placement?**
A: Yes – use the undo button or right-click/secondary action to remove items.

**Q: Are there limited items?**
A: No – you have unlimited access to all building elements.

**Q: Can I create multiple worlds?**
A: Yes – start a new project to create a different themed world.
