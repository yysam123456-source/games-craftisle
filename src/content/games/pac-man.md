# Pac-Man

**Category:** Arcade  
**Difficulty:** Medium

---

## Description

The most iconic arcade game of all time! Guide Pac-Man through a maze, eat all the dots, avoid the ghosts, and grab power pellets to turn the tables. Simple concept, timeless gameplay – Pac-Man defined an entire generation of gaming and remains just as fun today.

---

## How to Play Pac-Man

1. **Eat the Dots** – Use arrow keys (← ↑ ↓ →) to move Pac-Man through the maze. Eat all the small dots to complete the level.

2. **Avoid the Ghosts** – Four ghosts chase you:
   - **Blinky (Red)** – Chases Pac-Man directly
   - **Pinky (Pink)** – Tries to ambush by targeting ahead of Pac-Man
   - **Inky (Cyan)** – Unpredictable; his movement depends on other ghosts
   - **Clyde (Orange)** – Alternates between chasing and wandering

3. **Eat Power Pellets** – The four large flashing dots in the corners. When eaten, the ghosts turn blue and flee. Eat them for bonus points!

4. **Collect Fruit** – Periodic fruit appears in the center. Eat it for bonus points.

5. **Use the Tunnels** – The left and right sides of the maze are connected by tunnels. Escape through them to lose pursuing ghosts.

6. **Clear the Board** – Eat all dots to advance to the next level. Levels get faster!

## Tips & Strategies

- **Learn Ghost Behavior** – Understanding how each ghost moves helps you predict and avoid them.
- **Don't Corner Yourself** – Always have an escape route. Avoid dead ends.
- **Use Power Pellets Strategically** – Save them for when multiple ghosts are clustered together.
- **Clear One Section at a Time** – Focus on clearing one area before moving to the next.
- **The Tunnel Trick** – Ghosts slow down in tunnels. Use this to your advantage when being chased.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why do the ghosts sometimes change color?**
A: After eating a Power Pellet, ghosts turn blue and can be eaten. They flash when the effect is about to wear off.

**Q: What's the highest possible score?**
A: The game has a kill screen at level 256 where the maze is broken, effectively ending the game.

**Q: Who made Pac-Man?**
A: Pac-Man was created by Toru Iwatani at Namco and released in 1980. It became the highest-grossing arcade game of all time.
