# Slope - 3D Ball Racing Game

## 🎮 How to Play

Slope is a fast-paced 3D endless runner. You control a neon ball rolling down a narrow slope at high speed.

**Controls:**
- **Arrow Keys** or **A/D** - Steer left/right
- **Avoid red obstacles** - They make you crash
- **Don't fall off** - Stay on the track!

The game gets faster and faster. How long can you survive?

## 🏆 Tips & Strategy

1. **Stay centered** - The middle of the track gives you the most reaction time
2. **Don't overcorrect** - Small movements are better than sharp turns
3. **Watch the road ahead** - Look further down the track to anticipate curves
4. **Practice** - The more you play, the better you'll get at judging speed and distance

## 🎯 Game Modes

Currently Slope features endless mode - survive as long as possible and beat your high score!

## 📊 Scoring

- Survive longer = higher score
- Speed increases over time = more points per second
- Best score is saved locally
