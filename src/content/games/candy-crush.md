# Candy Crush

**Category:** Puzzle  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

The world's most popular match-3 puzzle game comes to your browser! Swap colorful candies to make matches of 3 or more, complete level objectives, and satisfy your sweet tooth. With hundreds of levels, special candy combinations, and addictive gameplay, Candy Crush is the perfect puzzle game for players of all ages.

---

## How to Play Candy Crush

1. **Swap Adjacent Candies** – Click or tap a candy, then drag it to an adjacent candy to swap positions.

2. **Make a Match** – If swapping creates a line of 3+ same-colored candies (horizontal or vertical), they match and disappear.

3. **Complete the Objective** – Each level has a different goal:
   - **Score X points** – Make matches to reach the target score
   - **Clear all jelly** – Match candies on top of jelly squares to remove the jelly
   - **Bring ingredients down** – Match candies below cherries/acorns to drop them to the bottom
   - **Clear all obstacles** – Destroy specified items (ice, chocolate, etc.)

4. **Create Special Candies**:
   - **4 in a row** → Striped Candy (clears a row or column when activated)
   - **5 in an L/T shape** → Wrapped Candy (explodes in a 3×3 area twice)
   - **5 in a row** → Color Bomb (removes all candies of one color when swapped)
   - **Striped + Wrapped combo** → Massive explosion
   - **Color Bomb + Striped combo** → Turns all striped candies of one color into striped and activates them

5. **Manage Your Moves** – Each level has a limited number of moves. Use them wisely!

## Tips & Strategies

- **Focus on the Bottom** – Making matches at the bottom causes cascading matches above, often creating free special candies.
- **Save Special Candies** – Don't activate them immediately. Combine them for massive effects.
- **Plan 2-3 Moves Ahead** – Look for setups that will create special candies.
- **Use Boosters Wisely** – Save boosters (lollipop hammer, striped lollipop, etc.) for levels you're stuck on.
- **Read the Objective Carefully** – Each level type requires a different strategy. Don't just blindly make matches.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many levels are there?**
A: The original game has thousands of levels, and new ones are added regularly.

**Q: What are the best combos?**
A: Color Bomb + Striped Candy is one of the most powerful combos in the game.

**Q: Can I play without spending money?**
A: Yes – the game is free. Money only buys boosters and extra lives.
