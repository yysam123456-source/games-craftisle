# Island Builder

**Category:** Building  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

Island Builder is a relaxing sandbox building game where you start with a small raft and sail to a deserted island. Gather resources, craft tools, and build your own island paradise from scratch. With no enemies or time pressure, it's the perfect game for creative players who love building and exploration.

---

## How to Play Island Builder

1. **Start Your Journey** – You begin on a small raft with basic tools. Sail toward the nearest island.

2. **Gather Resources** – Use your hands or tools to collect wood, stone, leaves, and food from trees, rocks, and the ocean.

3. **Craft Tools** – Open the crafting menu to create better tools like axes, pickaxes, and hammers that make gathering faster.

4. **Build Structures** – Place foundations, walls, roofs, and decorations to create houses, towers, bridges, and more.

5. **Expand Your Island** – As you progress, unlock new building materials and decorations. You can even terraform the landscape!

6. **Explore Nearby Islands** – Sail to other islands to find rare resources and inspiration for your builds.

## Tips & Strategies

- **Prioritize a Shelter First** – Build a basic hut before nightfall to stay safe and organized.
- **Collect Everything** – You never know when a specific resource will be needed for a recipe.
- **Plan Your Layout** – Mark out roads and zones before placing buildings for a cleaner look.
- **Use the Camera Mode** – Take screenshots of your builds to track progress and share with friends.
- **Experiment with Terraforming** – Raising and lowering terrain can create stunning waterfalls and gardens.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I play with friends?**
A: The web version is single-player only. Invite friends to visit your island by sharing screenshots!

**Q: Is there an end goal?**
A: No – Island Builder is a sandbox. Your goal is whatever you want it to be!

**Q: How do I get more building materials?**
A: Explore different islands – each has unique resources.
