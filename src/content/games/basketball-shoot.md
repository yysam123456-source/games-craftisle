# Basketball Shoot

**Category:** Sports  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

Test your basketball shooting skills in this fun and addictive sports game! Swipe to shoot the ball toward the hoop. Make as many baskets as possible within the time limit. Perfect your aim, master the physics, and become a three-point champion!

---

## How to Play Basketball Shoot

1. **Aim the Ball** – The ball follows your finger/mouse. Position it where you want to shoot from.

2. **Set Power and Angle** – Swipe up (mobile) or click and drag (desktop) to set the shot power and angle.

3. **Release to Shoot** – Let go to launch the ball toward the hoop.

4. **Score Points** – Make a basket to score:
   - **Normal shot** – 2 points
   - **3-pointer** (behind the arc) – 3 points
   - **Swish** (no backboard) – bonus points

5. **Beat the Timer** – You have a limited time to score as many points as possible.

6. **Use Limited Balls** – Some modes give you 10 balls. Missed shots count against your total.

## Tips & Strategies

- **Find Your Rhythm** – Each game has slightly different physics. Take a few practice shots to calibrate.
- **Aim for the Back of the Rim** – Shots that hit the back of the rim have a higher chance of going in.
- **Use the Backboard** – Bank shots are easier than swishes for beginners.
- **Don't Always Go for 3s** – Consistent 2-pointers beat missed 3-pointers.
- **Watch the Wind** – Some versions have wind that affects the ball trajectory. Adjust your aim accordingly.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How is the score calculated?**
A: Points depend on shot distance and whether you used the backboard. 3-pointers score more.

**Q: Can I play without time limit?**
A: Some versions offer a practice mode with no timer.

**Q: Are there different game modes?**
A: Many versions include time attack, limited balls, and challenge modes.
