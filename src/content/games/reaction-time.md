# Reaction Time Test

**Category:** Puzzle  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

How fast are your reflexes? The Reaction Time Test measures exactly how quickly you can respond to a visual stimulus. Wait for the screen to change color, then click as fast as you can! Compare your score with friends and see if you're above or below the human average of 250ms.

---

## How to Play Reaction Time Test

1. **Start the Test** – Click "Start" or wait for the test to begin.

2. **Wait for the Color Change** – The screen will be one color (usually red). Wait patiently.

3. **React Immediately** – As soon as the screen changes to green (or another target color), click or tap as fast as possible.

4. **Get Your Score** – Your reaction time is displayed in milliseconds (ms).

5. **Take Multiple Trials** – Most tests let you do 5-10 trials. Your final score is the average.

6. **Compare Your Score** – See how you rank:
   - **< 150ms** – eSports pro level
   - **150-200ms** – Excellent
   - **200-250ms** – Above average
   - **250-300ms** – Average
   - **> 300ms** – Room for improvement

## Tips & Strategies

- **Focus Completely** – Don't multitask or look away. Full attention is required.
- **Anticipate, But Don't Guess** – Trying to predict the color change leads to "false starts" (clicking too early).
- **Get in the Zone** – Take a few deep breaths before starting. Being relaxed helps.
- **Use a Mouse, Not Trackpad** – Physical mouse clicks register faster than trackpad taps.
- **Try Different Conditions** – Test yourself when well-rested vs. tired to see the difference.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What's the world record for reaction time?**
A: The fastest human reaction times are around 100-120ms, though these are hard to verify in controlled settings.

**Q: Why do I keep clicking too early?**
A: Anticipation is the enemy! Wait until you actually see the color change before moving.

**Q: Does age affect reaction time?**
A: Yes – reaction time typically peaks in your 20s and slows slightly with age, though training helps at any age.
