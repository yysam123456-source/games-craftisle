# CS Online

**Category:** Action  
**Difficulty:** Hard

---

## Description

CS Online brings the classic Counter-Strike experience to your browser! Join a team (Counter-Terrorists or Terrorists), plant or defuse the bomb, and eliminate the enemy team. With multiple maps, various weapons, and tactical gameplay, it's a great way to experience FPS action without installing anything.

---

## How to Play CS Online

1. **Join a Match** – Select a server and join either the Counter-Terrorist (CT) or Terrorist (T) team.

2. **Buy Weapons** – At the start of each round, use the buy menu to purchase weapons, armor, and grenades. Manage your in-game money wisely.

3. **Understand the Objective**:
   - **Bomb Defusal** – T's must plant the bomb at one of two bomb sites; CT's must prevent planting or defuse if planted.
   - **Team Deathmatch** – Eliminate the enemy team. Most kills wins.
   - **Hostage Rescue** – CT's must rescue hostages and bring them to safety.

4. **Master the Mechanics**:
   - **Aim** – Right-click or aim button for precision shots
   - **Shoot** – Left-click or fire button
   - **Reload** – R key or reload button
   - **Switch Weapons** – Number keys or weapon slot buttons
   - **Use Grenades** – Different grenades for different situations (flashbang, smoke, HE)

5. **Economy Management** – Winning rounds earns more money. Losing streaks get bonus compensation. Don't overspend early.

6. **Work as a Team** – Communicate with teammates, cover each other, and coordinate strategies.

## Tips & Strategies

- **Aim for the Head** – Headshots deal massive damage. Practice your aim!
- **Learn the Maps** – Knowing map layouts, common angles, and bomb sites is crucial.
- **Use Sound** – Footsteps reveal enemy positions. Wear headphones.
- **Don't Always Rush** – Sometimes holding an angle and waiting is better than pushing.
- **Buy Armor** – Kevlar saves lives. Always buy armor if you can afford it.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is this the same as CS:GO or CS2?**
A: This is a browser-based version inspired by Counter-Strike. It captures the core gameplay but is not the official game.

**Q: Can I play with friends?**
A: Some versions support multiplayer with other web players.

**Q: How do I improve my aim?**
A: Practice in aim training maps, lower your mouse sensitivity, and focus on crosshair placement.
