# Infinite Craft - Complete Guide

## 🎮 Game Overview

**Infinite Craft** is a mesmerizing element combination game that sparks creativity and curiosity. Starting with just four basic elements - Water 💧, Fire 🔥, Wind 🌬️, and Earth 🌍 - you'll combine them to discover hundreds of new elements, creatures, concepts, and even entire worlds!

This game is inspired by the original [Infinite Craft by Neal Agarwal](https://neal.fun/infinite-craft/), where the joy comes from experimentation and the thrill of discovery.

## 🎯 How to Play

### Basic Rules
1. **Drag elements** from the sidebar or canvas
2. **Drop them onto each other** to combine
3. **Discover new elements** when combinations are successful
4. **Keep experimenting** to unlock everything!

### Controls
- **Mouse**: Click and drag elements
- **Touch**: Tap and drag (mobile-friendly)
- **Keyboard**: Not required, but you can use Tab to navigate

## 💡 Pro Tips & Strategies

### 1. **Experiment Fearlessly**
- Try combining ANY two elements
- Some of the best discoveries come from unexpected combinations
- Don't be afraid to fail - there's no penalty!

### 2. **Think Thematically**
- Combine elements that make sense together (Fire + Earth = Lava)
- Think about real-world chemistry and physics
- Consider mythological or fantasy connections

### 3. **Build Complexity Gradually**
- Start with basic combinations
- Use your discoveries to create even more complex elements
- Work towards advanced concepts (Energy, Life, Magic)

### 4. **Organize Your Canvas**
- Keep related elements near each other
- Group discoveries by category (nature, technology, magic, etc.)
- Use the "Clear Canvas" button to start fresh

### 5. **Track Your Progress**
- Check the Discoveries panel to see what you've found
- Try to discover elements you haven't found yet
- Challenge yourself to find all 50+ combinations
