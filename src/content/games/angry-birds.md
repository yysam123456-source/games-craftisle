# Angry Birds

**Category:** Puzzle  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

The most downloaded mobile game of all time! Angry Birds is a physics-based puzzle game where you launch birds from a slingshot to destroy structures and defeat the green pigs hiding inside. Each bird has a unique ability – master them all to earn 3 stars on every level!

---

## How to Play Angry Birds

1. **Pull the Slingshot** – Click and drag backward on the slingshot to aim and set power.

2. **Release to Launch** – Let go to fire the bird toward the structures.

3. **Tap to Activate Bird Powers** – Each bird type has a special ability activated by tapping the screen mid-flight:
   - **Red Bird** – No special ability (basic bird)
   - **Blue Bird** – Splits into 3 smaller birds (tap to activate)
   - **Yellow Bird** – Speeds up and dives down (great for wood)
   - **Black Bird** – Explodes like a bomb (tap to detonate)
   - **White Bird** – Drops an egg bomb and flies upward
   - **Green Bird** – Boomerangs back (tap to reverse direction)
   - **Big Red Bird** – Very heavy, crashes through structures

4. **Destroy the Pigs** – The goal is to eliminate all green pigs. Structures can be used to your advantage – make them collapse on the pigs!

5. **Earn Stars** – Your score determines your star rating (1-3 stars per level). Higher scores = more stars.

6. **Use Limited Birds Wisely** – You have a limited set of birds per level. Don't waste them!

## Tips & Strategies

- **Aim for Weak Points** – Wood structures are weak at joints. Glass shatters easily. Stone is tough but vulnerable to bombs.
- **Use Gravity** – You don't always need to hit pigs directly. Destroying the support structure and letting gravity do the work is often more effective.
- **Save Special Birds** – Don't use your best birds on easy targets. Save them for tough structures.
- **Try Different Angles** – High arc vs. low flat trajectory can make a big difference.
- **Replay for 3 Stars** – If you only got 1 or 2 stars, replay the level with a better strategy to earn 3 stars.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many levels are there?**
A: The game has hundreds of levels across multiple themed worlds.

**Q: Which bird is best against which material?**
A: Yellow = wood, Black = stone, Red/Blue = glass (generally). But creativity matters more than type matching!

**Q: What are the golden eggs for?**
A: Golden Eggs unlock bonus levels with unique challenges.
