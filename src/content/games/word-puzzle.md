# Word Puzzle

**Category:** Puzzle  
**Difficulty:** Medium

---

## Description

Word Puzzle challenges your vocabulary and word-finding skills. Find hidden words in a grid of letters, solve crossword-style clues, or unscramble jumbled letters to form valid words. With thousands of puzzles and varying difficulty, it's the perfect brain workout for word lovers.

---

## How to Play Word Puzzle

*Note: Word Puzzle may refer to several game types. This guide covers the most common versions.*

### Word Search Mode:
1. **Find Hidden Words** – Words are hidden in the letter grid (horizontally, vertically, diagonally, forwards, or backwards).
2. **Select Letters** – Click/tap and drag across the letters to highlight a word.
3. **Words List** – Words to find are listed outside the grid. Cross them off as you find them.
4. **Complete the Grid** – Find all words to finish the puzzle.

### Word Scramble Mode:
1. **Unscramble Letters** – You're given jumbled letters (e.g., 'TAC' → 'CAT').
2. **Form Valid Words** – Rearrange the letters to spell a correct word.
3. **Submit Your Answer** – Enter the word and check if it's correct.
4. **Use Hints** – Stuck? Use a hint to reveal one letter.

### Crossword Mode:
1. **Read the Clues** – Each clue describes a word to fill in the grid.
2. **Type in Answers** – Click a cell and type your answer. Correct answers turn green.
3. **Use Cross-Referencing** – Solve easier clues first; intersecting letters help with harder ones.

## Tips & Strategies

- **Scan Methodically** – In word search, scan row by row rather than randomly.
- **Look for Rare Letters** – Q, X, Z, and J appear in fewer words. Find them first.
- **Build Vocabulary** – Reading regularly improves your word puzzle performance over time.
- **Use Online Tools Sparingly** – It's tempting to use a word solver, but solving yourself is more satisfying!
- **Practice Anagrams** – For word scramble, practice spotting common letter patterns.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Are the puzzles randomly generated?**
A: Most versions use a curated dictionary but shuffle puzzles for variety.

**Q: Can I play offline?**
A: Yes – once loaded, most word puzzle games work offline.

**Q: How many puzzles are there?**
A: Typically hundreds to thousands of puzzles, with new ones added regularly.
