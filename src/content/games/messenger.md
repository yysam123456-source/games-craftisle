# Messenger

**Category:** Arcade  
**Difficulty:** Medium

---

## Description

Messenger is a fast-paced platformer where you play as a messenger delivering letters across a dangerous world. Run, jump, slide, and fight your way through challenging levels filled with enemies and obstacles. With precise controls and progressive difficulty, it's a true test of skill and reflexes.

---

## How to Play Messenger

1. **Start Running** – Your messenger automatically runs forward. Tap to jump and double-jump.

2. **Dodge Obstacles** – Spikes, pits, and barriers will appear. Time your jumps carefully.

3. **Slide Under Gaps** – Swipe down or press the slide button to duck under low obstacles.

4. **Fight Enemies** – Attack enemies by jumping on them or using your weapon. Some enemies require multiple hits.

5. **Collect Letters** – Gather mail envelopes along the way – these are your score boosters.

6. **Use Power-ups** – Shields, magnets, and speed boosts appear randomly. Grab them for advantages.

7. **Reach the Goal** – Make it to the end of each level to unlock the next stage.

## Tips & Strategies

- **Listen to Audio Cues** – Many obstacles have sound warnings before they appear.
- **Don't Hold the Screen** – Tap precisely rather than holding; holding can cause accidental jumps.
- **Memorize Patterns** – Each level has repeating obstacle patterns. Learn them to improve.
- **Save Shields for Hard Sections** – Use shield power-ups when you see a difficult sequence ahead.
- **Practice the Boss Fights** – Boss patterns are consistent. Dying is part of learning!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is Morse Code required to play?**
A: No – Morse Code messages are optional lore. You can enjoy the game fully without deciphering them.

**Q: How many levels are there?**
A: The game features multiple worlds with increasing difficulty.

**Q: Can I play on mobile?**
A: Yes – the HTML5 version works great on mobile browsers with touch controls.
