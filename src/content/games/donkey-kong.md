# Donkey Kong

**Category:** Arcade  
**Difficulty:** Hard

---

## Description

Donkey Kong (1981) is the game that introduced Mario (then called Jumpman) to the world! Climb construction site ladders, dodge barrels thrown by the giant ape Donkey Kong, and rescue Pauline at the top. One of the most important games in history – and still incredibly fun today.

---

## How to Play Donkey Kong

1. **Move Mario** – Use arrow keys (← →) to walk and climb ladders.

2. **Jump** – Press Space or the jump button to jump. You can jump over barrels, fireballs, and gaps.

3. **Climb Ladders** – Walk into a ladder to climb up or down. Ladders are your main way to reach higher platforms.

4. **Dodge Barrels** – Donkey Kong throws barrels down the ramps. Dodge them by:
   - Jumping over them
   - Climbing a ladder to a different level
   - Using the hammer (see below)

5. **Grab the Hammer** – Periodically, a hammer appears. Pick it up to smash barrels and enemies for a few seconds. While holding the hammer, you can't climb ladders!

6. **Reach the Top** – Climb to the top of the construction site to rescue Pauline.

7. **Multiple Stages** – After rescuing Pauline, advance to the next stage with new challenges (conveyor belts, elevators, etc.).

## Tips & Strategies

- **Don't Rush Barrel Sections** – Wait for a clear path rather than jumping blindly.
- **Use the Hammer Wisely** – The hammer is powerful but limits your movement. Use it to clear a path, then drop it to climb.
- **Learn Barrel Paths** – Barrels follow predictable paths. Learn them to anticipate where to jump.
- **Watch Out for Fireballs** – After stage 1, fireballs appear. They move differently than barrels.
- **Practice the Jump Timing** – Mario's jump arc takes practice. Spend time in early levels mastering the feel.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many stages are there?**
A: The classic arcade version has 4 stages that repeat at higher difficulty.

**Q: Who made Donkey Kong?**
A: Shigeru Miyamoto created Donkey Kong. It was Nintendo's first major arcade hit and launched Mario's career.

**Q: What's the kill screen?**
A: At level 22 (in the original), a programming error makes the game impossible to complete. This is called the "kill screen".
