# Time Shooter 3

A first-person shooter with a unique twist - time only flows when YOU move. Stand still to freeze time and plan your shots perfectly.

## How to Play

- **WASD** to move
- **Mouse** to aim and shoot
- The slower you move, the slower time flows
- Dodge bullets by moving strategically
- Clear all enemies in each wave
