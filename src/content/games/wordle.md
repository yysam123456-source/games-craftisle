# Wordle

Guess the hidden 5-letter word in 6 attempts. Each guess reveals which letters are correct (green), in the wrong position (yellow), or not in the word at all (gray).

## How to Play

1. Type a valid 5-letter word and press Enter
2. Green = letter is correct and in the right position
3. Yellow = letter is in the word but wrong position
4. Gray = letter is not in the word
5. You have 6 guesses to find the word!
