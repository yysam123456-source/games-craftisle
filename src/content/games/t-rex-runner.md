# T-Rex Runner

**Category:** Arcade  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

The famous Chrome Dino game – now playable anytime without needing to go offline! Control a running T-Rex as you jump over cacti, duck under pterodactyls, and avoid obstacles in an endless desert run. How far can you go before the screen catches up to you?

---

## How to Play T-Rex Runner

1. **Start Running** – Press Space or tap the screen to start the game and make the T-Rex run.

2. **Jump Over Cacti** – Press Space or tap to jump. Small cacti require a normal jump; large cacti need the same timing.

3. **Duck Under Pterodactyls** – Press the down arrow or swipe down to duck when flying pterodactyls appear.

4. **Avoid Obstacles** – As speed increases, you'll encounter:
   - Small and large cacti (singly or in groups)
   - Pterodactyls flying at different heights
   - Mixed obstacles requiring both jumping and ducking

5. **Score Points** – Your score increases with distance. The game gets faster every 100 points.

6. **Night Mode** – The screen occasionally inverts to dark mode. This is cosmetic only.

7. **High Score** – Your best score is saved locally. Beat it!

## Tips & Strategies

- **Don't Hold the Key** – Tap quickly for precise jumps. Holding Space makes you jump too early next time.
- **Watch the Horizon** – Look ahead to spot obstacle combinations early.
- **Duck Early for Pterodactyls** – They appear suddenly. Duck as soon as you see the shadow.
- **Memorize Spacing** – Grouped cacti have consistent patterns. Learn to judge the gaps.
- **Stay Calm at High Speed** – The game is designed to be beatable at any speed. Keep your rhythm.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What's the maximum score?**
A: The game maxes out at 99,999 points, after which it resets but you can keep playing.

**Q: Why does the game speed up?**
A: Increasing speed is the core challenge – it tests your reaction time at higher levels.

**Q: Can I play on mobile?**
A: Yes – tap to jump, swipe down to duck. It works great on touchscreens.
