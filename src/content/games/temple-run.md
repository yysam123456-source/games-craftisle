# Temple Run

**Category:** Arcade  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Description

Temple Run is the game that defined the endless runner genre on mobile, now available in your browser! Swipe to turn, jump, and slide your way through an ancient temple while being chased by demon monkeys. Collect coins, unlock characters, and see how far you can run!

---

## How to Play Temple Run

1. **Start Running** – The game begins with your character automatically running forward.

2. **Swipe to Control**:
   - **Swipe Up / Space / W** – Jump over obstacles (tree roots, gaps, etc.)
   - **Swipe Down / Down Arrow / S** – Slide under overhead obstacles (branches, barriers)
   - **Swipe Left / A** – Turn left
   - **Swipe Right / D** – Turn right

3. **Collect Coins** – Gather gold coins along the path. They're used to buy upgrades and unlock characters.

4. **Grab Power-ups** – Special items appear on the path:
   - **Coin Magnet** – Attracts all nearby coins
   - **Shield** – Protects from one crash
   - **Boost** – Runs automatically at super speed
   - **Coin Bonus** – Multiplies coin value

5. **Fill the Power Meter** – Collect coins to fill the power meter. When full, you get a speed boost!

6. **Avoid Obstacles** – The path has gaps, tree roots, fire traps, and narrow bridges. React quickly!

## Tips & Strategies

- **Look Ahead** – Focus your eyes 2-3 seconds ahead of your character. Don't look at your feet.
- **Don't Over-Swipe** – One swipe per action is enough. Multiple swipes can cause accidental inputs.
- **Use the Shield Wisely** – Save shields for later in the run when obstacles come faster.
- **Upgrade the Coin Value** – Prioritize upgrading coin value in the store to progress faster.
- **Stay Calm at High Speed** – The game gets faster, but the obstacle patterns don't change. Keep your rhythm.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many characters can I unlock?**
A: The game features multiple unlockable characters, each with a slightly different appearance.

**Q: What's the best upgrade to buy first?**
A: Coin value upgrades give the best return on investment early on.

**Q: Can I play with a keyboard?**
A: Yes – use arrow keys or WASD to control your character.
